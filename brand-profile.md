## CTA preferences

- **Default tier rotation:** 60-70% soft, 20-30% medium, 10-15% direct.
- **Never two direct CTAs in a row.** Always alternate.
- **Wednesday personal stories: soft CTAs only.** Direct CTAs kill emotional resonance.
- **Always put newsletter link in the first comment.** Never in the post body (LinkedIn penalizes external links in body).
- **The CTA should feel like a friend mentioning something, not a marketer asking for something.**

See `references/cta-library.md` for the full decision tree and pattern library.
